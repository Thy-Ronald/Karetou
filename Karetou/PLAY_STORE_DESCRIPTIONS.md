**Karetou - Your Complete Guide to Silay City**

Discover, explore, and connect with the best of Silay City through Karetou - the all-in-one mobile app designed to help you navigate, discover local businesses, and earn rewards while exploring this beautiful heritage city.

**🗺️ Smart Navigation & Discovery**
• Find nearby businesses, restaurants, cafes, and attractions with ease
• Get turn-by-turn directions with multiple travel modes (driving, walking, cycling, transit)
• Real-time map view showing all businesses in your area
• Save your favorite places for quick access
• Discover suggested places and places to visit based on your location

• Track your points and transaction history
